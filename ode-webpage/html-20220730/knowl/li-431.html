Item 4.1.6.32.a.

Observe that

\begin{align*} a x_1'' + b_1' + cx_1 & = a \left(\frac{-b}{2a}\right)^2e^{-bt/2a} + b \left( \frac{-b}{2a} \right) e^{-bt/2a} + c e^{-bt/2a}\\ & = e^{-bt/2a} \left( \frac{b^2}{4a} - \frac{b^2}{2a} + c \right)\\ & = e^{-bt/2a} \left( \frac{-b^2 + 4ac}{4a} \right)\\ & = 0. \end{align*}
