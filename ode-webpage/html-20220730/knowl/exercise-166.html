Exercise 1.

Solve each of the following partially coupled systems.

  1. \begin{align*} x' & = 2x\\ y' & = x + 2y \end{align*}
  2. \begin{align*} x' & = -x + 3y\\ y' & = -2y \end{align*}
  3. \begin{align*} x' & = -3x\\ y' & = 2x + 3y \end{align*}
  4. \begin{align*} x' & = 2x - 3y\\ y' & = 4y \end{align*}
