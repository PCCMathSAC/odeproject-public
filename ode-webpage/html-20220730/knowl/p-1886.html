Suppose that the smell of a bunch of dead fish in the region \(-2 \leq x \leq 2\text{,}\) \(-2 \leq y \leq 2\) is given by the function

\begin{equation*} S(x, y) = x^2 + y^2 - \frac{x^4 + y^4}{4} - 3x^2 y^2 + 100. \end{equation*}

  1. What is the gradient system who vector field is the gradient of \(S\text{?}\)

  2. Use Sage to graph the phase portrait of the system.

  3. How many dead fish are there and where are they?

  4. Using the results from part (2), sketch the level sets of \(S\text{.}\)

  5. Why is this model not realistic for large values of \(x\) or \(y\text{?}\)
